MamaAlert
Automated Maternal Danger-Sign Screening

MamaAlert calls registered pregnant women on a schedule, in their own language, and asks six WHO-based danger-sign questions — no smartphone, app, or literacy required.

Each answer is scored automatically. If a call comes back red, a health worker is alerted immediately, closing the gap between a danger sign appearing and someone finding out about it.

The Six Danger Signs
Red flagVaginal bleeding
Red flagSevere headache or blurred vision
Red flagReduced or stopped fetal movement
Yellow flagFever
Yellow flagNew facial or hand swelling
Yellow flagUnusual abdominal pain
Color Coding
RED
Immediate health-worker alert
YELLOW

GREEN
